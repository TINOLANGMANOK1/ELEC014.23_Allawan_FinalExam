Reflections

Select an active panel node below to access the descriptive analytical documentation notes.

The visit was engaging and memorable — being on the studio floor during a live Balita Ngayon broadcast was eye-opening. Watching the crew coordinate live operations and use studio tools in real time turned abstract systems concepts into a vivid, practical experience.

For an aspiring IT professional, the tour highlighted how modern media relies on robust computing infrastructure. From synchronization systems to fiber routing, computing and networking support broadcasting at scale. Insights from alumni showed how software, network engineering, and hardware systems integrate to power large production environments.

Conclusion

This industry visit transformed classroom lessons into practical, hands-on experience within a fast-paced production environment. Seeing professionals operate complex systems gave me greater confidence as I prepare for a future in technology.

My sincere thanks to the UIC College of Computer Studies faculty, the Mindanao Media Hub engineering team, and the BSIT-2A cohort for organizing a safe, informative, and valuable tour.
